New Zealand Vehicle Registration & Trends Analysis with Tableau

Analysed nationwide vehicle registration data from New Zealand to uncover long-term trends in licensed vehicles and new registrations across categories, regions, and fuel types. Built interactive Tableau dashboards featuring KPI snapshots, year-on-year growth analysis, and regional drill-downs to support exploratory analysis and decision-making. The analysis identified Cars as the dominant vehicle category, with Auckland recording the highest vehicle volumes, largely driven by petrol-powered vehicles. This project demonstrates strong skills in data preparation, KPI design, filter-aware calculations, and translating complex transport data into clear, actionable insights.
